Regions - Veneto - Pinot-Grigio

About

Pinot Grigio is native to Burgundy France and was brought over to Italy during the 19th Century. It is the most popular white wine in Italy and accounts for 43% of the world’s production of Pinot Grigio. With its light and crisp taste, Pinot Grigio is very versatile and pairs well with so many dishes. Pinot Grigio is DOC recognized.
