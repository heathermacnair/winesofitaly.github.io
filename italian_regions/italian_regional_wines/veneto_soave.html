Regions - Veneto - Soave

About

Soave is a dry white wine native to Veneto and is also known as “Garganega”. There are five different types of Soave: Soave, Soave Spumante, Soave Colli Scaligeri, Soave Classico, and Ricito di Soave. They are all relatively similar except Recioto di Soave is significantly sweeter than the others. The different types of Soave mainy signify the different regions within Veneto in which the wines are produced. Soave varieties are known to pair well with a variety of complex dishes due to its high acidity and low alcohol content. Soave wine is also produced in Austria, Slovakia, Czech Republic, Hungary.
