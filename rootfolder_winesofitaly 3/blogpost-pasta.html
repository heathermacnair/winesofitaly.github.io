Pairing Wine with Pasta

Published November 11, 2020 by Heather MacNair

Pasta is a staple in Italian cusine that makes for an incredibly versitile and delicious meal! To perfect the dish, it is important to choose the right wine pairing that will inhance the flavors and complement the meal, specifically the sauce.

Use the guide below to see the best red and white wines that pair well with your favourite sauce!

Buon appetito!
