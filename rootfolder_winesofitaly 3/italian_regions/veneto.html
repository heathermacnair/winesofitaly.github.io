Regions - Veneto

Veneto

Veneto is a region that contains varying soil conditions that produce diverse grape varieties. As a result, Veneto is the largest wine producing region in the country, accounting for 20% of all Italian wines.

Red Wines:

  • Amarone
  • Bardolino
  • Valpolicella
  • Merlot

White Wines:

  • Soave
  • Pinot Grigio
  • Prosecco
  • Chardonnay

Popular Vineyards:

  • Villa Mosconi Bertani - Verona, Veneto
  • Adami - Treviso, Veneto
  • Villa Sandi - Triviso, Veneto
  • Le Vigne di Alice - Triviso, Veneto
