Regions - Sicily

Sicily

Sicily is the largest island in the Mediterranean Sea and is one of the most dynamic wine industries in Europe. Its warm and dry climate with mild rainfall promotes the perfect environment for wine cultivation because it prevents the chances of vineyard pests and disease.
