Pairing Wine with Pasta

Published January 1, 2021 by Heather MacNair
Share
Tweet

Pasta is a staple in Italian cusine that makes for an incredibly versitile and delicious meal! To perfect the dish, it is important to choose the right wine pairing that will inhance the flavors and complement the meal, specifically the sauce. Use the guide below to see the best red and white wines that pair well with your favourite sauce! Buon appetito!

Pasta Sauce White Wines Red Wines
Tomato Sauce Pinot Grigio, Verdicchio Chianti, Montepulciano d'Abruzzo, Nebbiolo
Cream Sauce Prosecco, Bianco di Custoza, Lugana, Chardonnay light Merlot, Pinot Noit
Seafood Sauce Vernaccia di San Gimignano, Pinot Grigio Chiaretto
Pesto Sauce Soave, Gruner Veltliner, Gavi Sangiovese, Pinot Noir, Merlot
Spicy Sauce Asti Valpolicella Ripasso, Lambrusco, Primitivo
